Project Overview

Krishiva Tech is a modern web and mobile software development company offering services such as web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing. The objective of this project was to design a conversion-focused landing page that clearly communicates the company’s expertise, services, and credibility while encouraging potential clients to get in touch or request a quote.

Target Users

The application is primarily designed for:

  • Individuals looking for a reliable technology partner to build or scale digital products.
  • Businesses seeking professional web, mobile, or digital marketing solutions to grow their online presence.
  • Managers or executives evaluating service providers for long-term development partnerships.

Company Requirement

The company needed a landing page that effectively communicates its value proposition and builds trust within seconds. Without strong structure, clear messaging, and strategic CTAs, potential clients may leave before understanding the services or credibility of the brand.

UX Challenges

From a UX perspective, the key challenges were:

  • Presenting multiple services without overwhelming users.
  • Communicating technical expertise in a simple, accessible way.
  • Establishing trust quickly through visuals and structure.
  • Designing strong CTAs to drive inquiries and quote requests.
  • Creating a professional and modern design aligned with industry standards.

Project Goals

To address the identified problems, the following solutions were implemented:

  • Design a modern, professional landing page that reflects technical expertise.
  • Clearly communicate the company’s value proposition within the first few seconds.
  • Structure services and content for easy scanning.
  • Build trust using testimonials, portfolio sections, and technology stack highlights.
  • Increase user engagement and inquiry conversions.

The Solution

The solution was built around reducing complexity and creating a more intuitive vendor experience.

  • Designed a strong hero section with a clear headline, supporting copy, and prominent CTA.
  • Structured services into clean, digestible sections with consistent visual hierarchy.
  • Integrated testimonials and portfolio previews to build trust.
  • Created a streamlined contact section with a clear “Get in Touch” or “Request a Quote” flow.
  • Applied consistent typography, spacing, and modern UI patterns to ensure professionalism.
