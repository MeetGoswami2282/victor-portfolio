Universal Point of Sale (UPOS)

Company

Global Garner

Role

UI/UX Designer

Platform

Landing Page

Year

2023

Project Overview

UPOS (Universal Point of Sale) is a digital platform designed to help local business owners and entrepreneurs start and grow their businesses by offering financial services and digital tools. The project focused on redesigning the existing landing page (old design) into a modern, conversion-focused experience (new design) that clearly communicates UPOS’s value proposition, improves usability, and drives trial sign-ups.

Target Users

The application is primarily designed for:

  • Retailers and shop owners looking for digital tools and financial services to grow their business.
  • Individuals exploring POS and financial solutions to manage transactions and earnings.
  • Users evaluating UPOS as a platform to distribute services and expand reach.

Problem Statement

The original landing page lacked clarity, structure, and conversion focus. Important information was scattered, visual hierarchy was weak, and primary CTAs were not prominent. As a result, users struggled to quickly understand what UPOS offers and why they should sign up.

UX Challenges

From a UX perspective, the key challenges were:

  • Improving visual hierarchy to guide user attention effectively.
  • Simplifying complex service offerings into clear, digestible sections.
  • Making primary CTAs more visible and action-driven.
  • Reducing cognitive load caused by dense text and poor grouping.

Project Goals

To address the identified problems, the following solutions were implemented:

  • Transform the outdated layout into a clean, modern SaaS-style landing page.
  • Clearly communicate UPOS’s benefits and earning potential.
  • Improve content structure for better scanability.
  • Strengthen primary and secondary CTAs to increase conversions.
  • Establish credibility through better layout, visuals, and trust indicators.

The Solution

The solution was built around reducing complexity and creating a more intuitive vendor experience.

  • Introduced a strong hero section with clear headline, supporting copy, and prominent primary CTA.
  • Reorganized content into logical sections (Benefits, Earning Model, Features, Plans, Trust Indicators).
  • Reduced text-heavy areas and improved spacing for better readability.
  • Designed a clear pricing section with easy comparison.
  • Strengthened CTAs throughout the page to guide users toward trial and signup.
  • Implemented consistent typography, spacing, and illustration style for a modern SaaS experience.
